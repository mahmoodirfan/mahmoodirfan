# Hi, I'm Irfan Mahmood

I work at the intersection of remote sensing, GIS, geophysics, climate science, and environmental monitoring. My interests center on turning Earth observation data into practical insight for natural hazards, climate impacts, marine and coastal systems, and spatial decision-making.

## What I Focus On

- Remote sensing and GIS for environmental and climatic studies
- Natural hazards monitoring and risk assessment
- Climate change impacts, marine heatwaves, and environmental variability
- Geospatial data processing, spatial visualization, and modeling
- Research workflows that connect satellite data, field knowledge, and reproducible analysis

## Tools And Methods

- Google Earth Engine, ArcGIS, QGIS, ENVI, SNAP
- Python, geospatial analysis, data visualization, and scientific computing
- Satellite products, climate datasets, time-series analysis, and spatial modeling
- Research communication through maps, figures, reports, and publications

## Collaboration

I am open to research and applied projects involving climate change monitoring, environmental analysis, geospatial analytics, natural hazards, and innovative uses of remote sensing technologies. I enjoy collaborating with researchers, developers, and organizations working to make Earth observation data more useful for people and ecosystems.
